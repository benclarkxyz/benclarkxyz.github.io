Xcode Indentation Failures and Fixes

Proper indentation in coding is important to me. It aids readability and makes the code much more pleasant to look at and easier to read. That is why I constantly use Xcode's Re-indent command, i.e. Editor -> Structure -> Re-indent or ctrl I. However, this comment add's a tab to tags that do not have a closing tag. Therefore the <link> and <meta> tags that are common at the top of an html document will cause the rest of the document to more indented than nessasary.

To fix this

/Applications/Xcode.app/Contents/SharedFrameworks/DVTFoundation.framework/Resources

Then find html.xclangspec and JavaScript.xclangspec
