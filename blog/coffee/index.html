Half-life of Caffeine

It is useful to understand the half-life of caffeine for several reasons. It can help you better control your tolerance, addition, and accumulation of caffeine in the body. Caffeine is a complex drug and the human body is fare more so. So of course it will effect everyone differently, but it seems that for most people the half-life of caffeine is about 6 hours.
